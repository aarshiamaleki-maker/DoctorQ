Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it.

1 Introduction

Welcome to DoctorQ. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By using DoctorQ, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

DoctorQ is an appointment booking and queue management tool only. We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

2 Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register and use DoctorQ:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • City of residence
  • Appointment details (doctor name, date, time, queue number)
  • Doctor or clinic information (for healthcare provider accounts)

We do not collect any sensitive medical records, diagnoses, prescriptions, or health history.

3 How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Booking and confirming appointments
  • Generating QR codes for clinic check-in
  • Managing live queue positions in real time
  • Allowing clinic staff to identify patients during check-in
  • Improving app performance and user experience

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

4 Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely using Apple CloudKit, Apple's cloud infrastructure. Your data is stored in Apple's servers and is subject to Apple's security standards.

  • Passwords are encrypted using SHA-256 hashing before storage
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • We do not store passwords in plain text under any circumstances
  • Access to your data is restricted to your account only

Your data is stored on Apple's iCloud infrastructure. Apple's privacy policy also applies. Visit apple.com/privacy for more information.

5 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. Your information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With the clinic or doctor you have booked an appointment with — so they can identify you during check-in
  • With Apple as required to operate CloudKit services
  • If required by law or legal process

No advertising networks, data brokers, or marketing companies ever receive your data.

6 Camera Usage

DoctorQ requests access to your device camera solely for the purpose of scanning patient QR codes during the clinic check-in process. This feature is used exclusively by healthcare provider accounts.

We do not store, transmit, or process any images or video captured by the camera. Camera access is only active while the QR scanner is open.

7 iCloud Requirement

DoctorQ requires an active iCloud account on your device to function. This is because we use Apple CloudKit for secure data storage. We do not access your personal iCloud files, photos, contacts, or any other iCloud data beyond what DoctorQ stores itself.

8 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion — you can request that we delete your account and all associated data
  • Right to correction — you can request that we correct any inaccurate data
  • Right to portability — you can request your data in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

9 Children's Privacy

DoctorQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of DoctorQ after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11 Medical Disclaimer

DoctorQ is an appointment booking and queue management tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified and licensed healthcare professional for any medical concerns.
