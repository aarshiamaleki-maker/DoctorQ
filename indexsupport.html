How DoctorQ Works

1
Sign up as a Patient or Clinic Choose your role on the welcome screen and create your account.
2
Find a Doctor & Book Browse doctors by specialty, pick a date and time slot, and confirm your booking.
3
Get Your QR Code On the day of your appointment, open the booking and tap "Activate & Show QR".
4
Check In at the Clinic Show your QR code to the receptionist who scans it to add you to the live queue.
5
Track Your Queue in Real Time Watch your queue position update automatically every 10 seconds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using DoctorQ.

For Patients
Make sure you are signed into iCloud on your device. Go to iPhone Settings → tap your name at the top → make sure you are signed in with your Apple ID. DoctorQ uses iCloud to store data securely. Also double check your email and password are entered correctly — passwords are case sensitive.
Pull down on the My Appointments screen to refresh. If the booking still does not appear, it may not have saved correctly due to a network issue. Try booking again and make sure you have a stable internet connection. If the problem persists, contact us at aarshiamaleki@gmail.com.
The QR code only appears on the day of your appointment. If it is not the day of your appointment yet, you will see a "Not Yet Active" message. On the appointment day, tap "Activate & Show QR" to generate your code.
To cancel an appointment, please contact us directly at aarshiamaleki@gmail.com with your name and appointment details. We will process the cancellation for you. We are working on adding an in-app cancellation feature in a future update.
Currently there is no automatic password reset in the app. Please email us at aarshiamaleki@gmail.com with the email address you registered with and we will reset your password manually within 24 hours.
The live queue updates automatically every 10 seconds. Make sure you have an active internet connection. If the queue appears stuck, close the appointment detail screen and reopen it to force a fresh update.
This can happen if your internet connection is slow or unstable. Wait a few seconds for CloudKit data to load. You can also tap the refresh button in the top right corner of the home screen. If no doctors appear after refreshing, it may mean no doctors have registered in your area yet.
For Clinics & Doctors
On the welcome screen, select "I'm a Doctor". Fill in your clinic details and enter the Clinic Access Code provided by DoctorQ. If you do not have an access code, contact us at aarshiamaleki@gmail.com to request one.
Make sure you have granted camera permission to DoctorQ. Go to iPhone Settings → DoctorQ → Camera → turn on. Also ensure the patient's QR code is well lit and hold the phone steady 15–30 cm from the screen for best results.
Wait 10–15 seconds and pull down to refresh the queue. The queue updates via CloudKit which may take a few seconds. If the patient still does not appear, ask them to show you their booking ID and contact us at aarshiamaleki@gmail.com with the details.
Go to the Profile tab in your clinic dashboard. Under "Block Out Date", select the date you want to block and tap "Confirm Day Off". Patients will not be able to book appointments on that date.
After registering, your clinic should appear to patients within a few minutes. If it does not appear, make sure you have a stable internet connection and try logging out and back in. Contact us at aarshiamaleki@gmail.com if the issue continues.
Technical
DoctorQ uses Apple CloudKit to securely store and sync appointment data between patients and clinics in real time. This requires an active iCloud account. We do not access any of your personal iCloud data — only the data that DoctorQ itself creates and stores.
Yes. All data is stored using Apple CloudKit with encrypted connections. Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 before being stored — we never store plain text passwords. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
To delete your account and all associated data, email us at aarshiamaleki@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and the email address you registered with. We will permanently delete all your data within 7 business days and confirm via email.
